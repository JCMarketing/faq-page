Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Is this platform available in Malaysia? What currency is used?
Yes, our platform is available in Malaysia. All transactions are processed in Malaysian Ringgit (MYR).
Q2: How can I deposit funds?
Once logged in, go to the Deposit page, choose your preferred payment method, and follow the instructions.
Q3: How do I register an account??
Click on the Register button, enter your mobile number, verification code, and create a password. It only takes a minute.
Q4: What deposit methods are available? How do I deposit?
We support online banking (FPX), DuitNow, Touch'n Go EWallet etc. After logging in, go to the Deposit page and follow the instructions.
Q5: How do I withdraw funds? How long does it take?
Go to the Withdraw section, enter your bank account details and amount. Withdrawals are usually processed within 5–15 minutes.
Q6: What should I do if my withdrawal fails?
Ensure your bank details are correct and that you've met all bonus turnover requirements. If it still fails, please contact our Live Chat support.
Q7: How can I check my transaction history?
Log in and go to History to view all your deposits, and withdrawals.
Q8: Is my personal information secure?
Absolutely. We use SSL encryption and follow strict data privacy measures to protect your information.
Q9: What should I do if I forget my password?
Click Forgot Password on the login page. You can reset it using your registered mobile number.
Q10: Can I earn commission by referring friends?
Yes! You can earn up to 5% lifetime commission and 30% Referral Bonus by referring friends. The more they play, the more you earn.
Q11: Will I receive my winnings automatically?
Yes, all winnings will be automatically credited to your account after the game is settled.
Q12: How do I contact customer support?
Click the Live Chat button on our website. Our support team is available 24/7.
Q13: What is rollover? What is turnover?
Rollover/ Win Over: The total amount you need to wager before you can withdraw your bonus or winnings. Turnover/ Total Bet: The total value of your bets, win or lose, within a period..
